Welcome to HAPS Hiranagar Campus

Him Academy Public School Hiranagar is a day cum boarding school. This campus is located at Hiranagar Hamirpur, just one kilometre away from main bus stand Hamirpur, connected by road. Hiranagar is a posh colony of Hamirpur on the outskirts of it. The school is very close to circuit house, Hamirpur. It is a beautiful, neat and clean campus with a lovely homely feel. It runs classes from Nursery to 10+2. The school has only Medical and Non-Medical groups in 10+1 & 10+2 classes.
